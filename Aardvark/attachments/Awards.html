Awards and Personal Recognition

Most of the personal awards I've received have come due to my company PowerQuest. This was an incredible ride, and I couldn't have done it without some amazing people surrounding me, including my brother Dave and the rest of my wonderful intial team: Robert Raymond, Michael Robertson, Troy Millet, Ron Hammond, and John Christensen.

And our amazing team was added upon many times. We were able to attract and hire other talented professionals who joined our company... and then became wonderful friends. We would say how grateful we were for such a great situation with WordPerfect and Novell close by: they would find, hire, and train the most incredible talent... then we would steal them and make 'em ours!

Intellectual Property
